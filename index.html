Everything About Your Rugby CV

Sign up free, complete the 8-step onboarding wizard with your combine stats (Bronco time, sprint splits, strength data), playing history and a highlight video clip. Your shareable URL is live within 10 minutes. Scouts at Premiership and URC clubs can find you immediately through the Scout Board.
The key metrics are: Bronco test (20-40-60m shuttle), 10m and 40m sprint times, VO₂ max, and your three main lifts (bench press, back squat, deadlift). Height, weight and standing reach complete the physical picture. Having verified combine data increases scout enquiries significantly compared to profiles without it.
Scouts use the Scout Board to filter players by position, age, height, Bronco time, passport eligibility and video availability. Verified profiles with complete combine data rank at the top. Over 340 clubs currently have scout access, including Premiership, URC, Top 14, Pro D2 and Super Rugby academies.
A digital rugby passport goes further than a traditional PDF CV. It includes a verified, shareable URL that works on any device, live combine stats, an embedded video reel, passport and visa eligibility chips, and real-time analytics showing who has viewed your profile. Clubs can see everything without downloading anything.
EU passport eligibility is one of the most commercially valuable attributes in European club rugby. Top 14, URC and Premiership clubs are bound by overseas player quotas — an EU-eligible player does not count against these limits. Players who clearly display their passport eligibility on their profile receive significantly more enquiries from European clubs.
